Factorial Problem
Input: numberIn

Processing: counter

Output: factorial

Algorithm:
1.  enter the numberIn
2.  if (numberIn is greater than or equal to 2 AND numberIn is less than or equal to 60)
	repeat for (counter = 1, counter less than or equal to numberIn, in increments of 1)
		factorial = factorial * counter
	end repeat
	display factorial
    end if
3. display error message
End
